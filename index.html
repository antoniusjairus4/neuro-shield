01 — Understanding the Disease

What is Alzheimer's Disease?

A progressive neurological disorder causing brain cells to degenerate — the leading cause of dementia, affecting memory, cognition, and behavior at global scale.

// 01

The Disease Mechanism

Alzheimer's causes progressive accumulation of amyloid plaques and tau tangles in the brain, disrupting neural signaling pathways and leading to irreversible cognitive decline over time.

// 02

The Scale of the Crisis

Over 55 million people live with dementia globally. Without early detection, progression goes unnoticed for years — costing lives, families, and healthcare systems enormously.

// 03

Why MRI is the Key

MRI scans reveal hippocampal atrophy and cortical thinning — structural biomarkers that signal Alzheimer's onset years before symptoms emerge clinically. AI makes this detection instant.

// 04 — Critical Window

The Detection Gap

Current average diagnosis delay is 2–3 years from onset. NeuroShield AI closes this gap — providing neurologists with probabilistic confidence scores across 4 disease stages from a single MRI scan upload.

02 — The AI Pipeline

How NeuroShield Works

A five-stage clinical AI workflow — from secure MRI upload to explainable deep learning output and doctor-confirmed diagnosis.

Step 01 — Input
Secure MRI Upload

Neurologist uploads DICOM or NIfTI brain MRI through an encrypted, HIPAA-compliant portal. Instant queue assignment and transmission confirmation.

Step 02 — Preprocessing
Medical-Grade Normalization

Skull stripping, bias field correction, and spatial normalization to MNI atlas — ensuring consistent input quality regardless of scanner or acquisition protocol.

Step 03 — Inference
ResNet50 / EfficientNet Analysis

Our ensemble deep learning model analyzes hippocampal volume, cortical thickness, and white matter patterns — trained on 10,000+ annotated scans across all 4 disease stages.

Step 04 — Explainability
Grad-CAM Heatmap + Confidence Report

A probabilistic confidence score and spatial heatmap highlight regions driving the prediction — explainable AI that builds clinical trust and supports radiologist review.

Step 05 — Clinical Action
Doctor Reviews, Confirms & Acts

The report is saved to the patient profile. Neurologist adds notes, confirms or overrides the AI decision, and shares the final diagnosis — complete audit trail maintained.
